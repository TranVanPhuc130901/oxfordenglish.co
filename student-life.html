Social Activities

Make friends and have fun with our exciting social programme!

Download
For many of our students their journey to Oxford to study at the language school represents a major change of lifestyle. It is vitally important therefore that the language school offers expert help and guidance regarding all aspects of student life. Even before our overseas students arrive in the UK our team are working hard on their behalf to ensure that all goes well on their arrival. The team are well aware that students’ academic success at the language school will often depend on their comfort in their surroundings and their ability to overcome any problems they may have. Oxford’s strength and success comes from its academic excellence and its diversity and unique mix of cultures. The city is constantly changing – retaining a sense of tradition while also embracing the new. Student life in Oxford is not just about your study – it’s about the friends you make and the time you spend exploring one of the most fascinating cities in the world.
